## Featured Snippet Opportunities

Target these with FAQ schema and direct answer formats:

1. **"How long should you stay in a sauna?"**
   - Answer format: "Beginners: 5-10 minutes, Intermediate: 15-20 minutes, Advanced: 20-30 minutes. Research shows 3-7 sessions per week of 15-20 minutes yields optimal health benefits."

2. **"Does sauna help with weight loss?"**
   - Answer format: "Saunas cause temporary water weight loss (not fat loss). Average person loses 1 pint of fluid per session, regained with hydration. Indirect benefits: better recovery, improved sleep, stress reduction may support weight management."

3. **"Is sauna good for skin?"**
   - Answer format: "Yes. Research shows 15-20 minute infrared sessions 5x/week for 6 months improves wrinkles 20-50%. Sauna increases collagen production, improves circulation, and cleanses pores. Hydrate after to prevent dehydration."

4. **"Sauna before or after workout?"**
   - Answer format: "After workout is better for recovery. Studies show post-exercise sauna reduces muscle soreness, improves neuromuscular performance. Wait 30-60 minutes after training, session length 15-20 minutes at 140-190°F."

## AI Search Optimization Strategy

### How to Get Cited by Perplexity/ChatGPT/Claude

1. **Cite Primary Research**
   - Link to PubMed studies, Mayo Clinic research
   - Include specific statistics: "50% reduced cardiovascular risk"
   - Reference Finnish 20-year study with 2,300+ participants

2. **Structure for AI Parsing**
   - Clear H2/H3 hierarchy
   - Bullet points for key facts
   - Data tables for comparisons
   - FAQ sections with schema markup

3. **Establish E-E-A-T**
   - Medical professional review badges
   - Author credentials in bio
   - Last updated dates
   - Original research/surveys when possible

4. **Be Balanced & Honest**
   - Acknowledge limitations ("More research needed on...")
   - Include contraindications and safety warnings
   - Don't overhype benefits
   - Cite conflicting studies when they exist
